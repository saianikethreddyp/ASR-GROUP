About ASR Group

Experience measured in work delivered.

ASR is an interiors and construction group based in Hyderabad, with experience across residential, corporate, retail, institutional and public environments. Over more than two decades, the practice has expanded its capabilities to connect design, engineering, production and execution within a coordinated delivery system.

From interior execution to integrated delivery.

ASR's journey began with interiors and the belief that a successful space depends on more than its appearance. Material knowledge, technical coordination, production control and site discipline all shape the final result.

As the scale and complexity of its work grew, ASR expanded into construction and related capabilities. Today, the group can coordinate architecture, structural expertise, joinery, glazing, metalwork, project management and execution around a shared project objective.

What Guides Us

Quality

Quality is established through decisions, details, materials, coordination and inspection—not through a final layer of decoration.

Accountability

Responsibility should remain clear throughout the project. Clients should know who is coordinating the work and what happens next.

Partnership

Strong projects depend on open communication between the client, design team, consultants, suppliers and site teams.

Continuous improvement

ASR continues to invest in people, processes, production capabilities and technical knowledge.

Leadership grounded in delivery.

Under the leadership of Managing Director A. S. Ram Raju, ASR has developed its interiors and construction capabilities around planning, partnership and long-term performance.

"Our responsibility is not limited to creating a strong design. It is to protect that intent through every decision required to build it."

— A. S. Ram Raju, Managing Director
